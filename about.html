India Gate Downtown has been serving you the authentic Indian cuisine for years. With many years of journey in the hospitality industry, India Gate Downtown has made a mark for itself in Miami, Florida. At India Gate Downtown we have a wide variety of menu and with all our Indian exotic ingredients, unfamiliar dishes, and tongue-tingling flavors, our Indian cuisine can be both exciting and intimidating. we aim to provide an everlasting impression on ones’s mind by providing outstanding service and authentic taste of traditional Indian cuisines. Our dishes are prepared with the freshest and finest of ingredients, fresh herbs & spices to bring you some lip-smacking yet healthy delicacies. Our dishes are made with great attention to the recipes keeping in mind the authentic taste and flavours of the cuisine. We have great options for vegetarian and non-vegetarian choices, and also for your snack time and meals. The easiest way of bringing the tastes of India into your mouth is by ordering online for Takeout or India Gate Downtown delivery at your door steps.
